ABOUT US

Word Impact Nation (WIN) is a Ghanaian youth-focused Christian ministry led by Pastor Benjamin Ansah, its founder and Global Lead Pastor. The ministry is centered on spiritual growth, leadership development, and building a community of passionate young believers. WIN is active digitally through YouTube, podcasts, and Instagram, where they share teachings, events, and outreach activities. Their programs include youth services, encounter nights, and inspirational messages designed to empower young people to grow in faith and purpose.
